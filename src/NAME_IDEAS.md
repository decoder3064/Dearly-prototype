# 🌿 Name Ideas for Your Slow-Tech Letter Project

## Concept Overview
A fake mailer that creates anticipation through delayed delivery, encouraging intentional communication and deeper connections. Features include handwritten/digital letters, package tracking integration, and intimate 1-to-1 or small group correspondence.

---

## Top Recommendations

### **1. Slowpost** ⭐
*Simple, memorable, immediately communicates the concept*
- Clean, modern, tech-startup feel
- ".slowpost" could be a unique domain
- Tagline: "Letters worth waiting for"

### **2. Drift** ⭐⭐
*Evokes the slow journey of a letter traveling*
- Intimate, poetic, single-word impact
- "Your letter is drifting to them..."
- Works well for: "Drift Mail," "On Drift"

### **3. Wayfinder**
*Suggests journey, anticipation, and meaningful direction*
- Tracking/navigation metaphor built-in
- Premium, thoughtful feeling
- "Your letter is finding its way..."

### **4. Tender**
*Double meaning: tender as in gentle, and tender as in mail carrier*
- Intimate, warm, vulnerable
- "Tender Letters" or just "Tender"
- Emphasizes the emotional intentionality

### **5. LongLetter** ⭐
*Plays on "long distance" and taking time*
- Self-explanatory concept
- "The long way is the meaningful way"
